our teachers

Our teachers are all professionals in their fields and trained facilitators. Our role in class is to navigate and bring to light the most effective discussion, support everyone in the room to understand our common objective and assist the planning of how to achieve these objectives.

brenna dickinson

Founder of Tridrama

Finished with a 1st Class honours in Drama, Applied Theatre and Education at The Royal Central School of Speech and Drama. Has curated and conducted several outreach projects in different countries including South Africa, Honduras and Indonesia.

what we offer

We offer an adaptable curriculum specially designed to fit particular needs of our participants, as well as the demands of our current times. Our content is inclusive, engaging, informed, balanced and alert. Together we will develop our interpersonal skills, grow our confidence, expand our awareness and make many friends in the process.

Oh and of course, have an enjoyable time doing it!

outreach work

TRi Drama was created as an outreach company developing performing arts programs and other drama projects specific for those underserved by their society, and for those who do not have the opportunity for alternative education or education at all. Past-projects include work in South Africa, Honduras and Indonesia. We are always searching for new people and communities to work with so please contact us if you are interested in setting up a project.
